    What is a Click Speed Test?

    A click speed test measures how many times you can click within a specific time period. The most popular version is the 10 second CPS test.

    How is CPS calculated?

    CPS stands for Clicks Per Second. It is calculated by dividing the total number of clicks by the total time in seconds.

    Why train your clicking speed?

    Improving your click speed can help in competitive gaming, reaction time training, and improving mouse control skills.

    How to get a higher CPS score?

    • Use your index finger
    • Keep your wrist relaxed
    • Use butterfly clicking technique
    • Practice daily
